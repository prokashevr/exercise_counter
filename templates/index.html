🏃 Jumping Jack Counter

How to Use

  • Stand back so your full body is visible to the webcam
  • Raise your arms above your shoulders for "UP" stage
  • Lower your arms below your shoulders for "DOWN" stage
  • Complete a full repetition: Down → Up → Down
  • Counter resets automatically after 7 seconds of inactivity
  • Reach 50 and 100 reps for special signals!
